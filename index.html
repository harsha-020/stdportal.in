About the COLLEGE

JPKP was established in 2013 by Sri Jyothi Educational Society under Sri Venkata Ramana who has a over 40+ years majestic experience in teaching field. JPKP Offers diploma in conventional and advanced branches for its student's . JPKP has strong line of experienced staff through out its organisational line.
